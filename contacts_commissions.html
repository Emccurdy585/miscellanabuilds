Commission

Interested in hiring me for a commission? Look no further! As of now, I am open for commissions. Send an email to miscellanabuilds@gmail.com with a title of "Commission Inquiry" (or something similar, I read them all anyways!). I will reach back as soon as possible and we can start the process!


Want to know more about the process first? Then read on!

Craft a...

Concept

Every commission starts with a concept. Make sure to have an idea of what the commission will be about. It could be vague, such as just a Character Build of some kind, or it could be specific, such as a specific scene from a movie. In either case, we will work together to figure out the perfect objective!

Specify a...

Scope

The next step in the process is determining the scope of the project. There are two main components of this: size and delivery method. Do you want a small desktop model? Or something large to be displayed? Do you simply want a digital model that you can buy the pieces for and figure out how to put it together? Or perhaps you want the full physical model mailed to you?

Develop a...

Design

Once the foundations are laid, it's time to get designing! I will work with you to establish an initial concept. Depending on the topic, it could start as basic as conversation and sketches or by jumping right into build sketches. This usually depends on how much creative freedom there is in the project. Replicating a character or room is a lot less design-heavy as creating one from scratch!
