Builder Improvement Initiative

Founded in 2020, Builder Improvement Initiative was a product of a time where Lego communities failed at cultivating a community where people could grow in their skills. BII is devoted to just that. With almost 500 members, you're bound to find someone knowledgeable to give you some advice on how to improve your builds!

Join the server!

Ninjago Kickback

In 2023 I had the chance to work with a fellow RebelLUG member to plan a collaboration called Ninjago Kickback! In this collab, 10 participants each recreated an iconic set from the initial Ninjago wave in 2010. More information to come soon once the collab is posted!
